How It Works

WebAR Flow

  1. Upload one or more target images and matching MP4 videos.
  2. The dashboard compiles all target images into a single MindAR `.mind` file.
  3. Open the AR viewer on a phone, allow camera access, and point at one of the uploaded images.
  4. The mapped video appears anchored over the detected image and plays in a loop.
